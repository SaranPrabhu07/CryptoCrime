// I am not what I cook, yet I feed what builds.
— LAYER I —
They say I smell of kitchens and boiling vats,
that I am the domain of proteins and fats.
The hungry athlete knocks on my door,
yet I have never plated a single meal before.

A biologist calls me edible,

I study what fuels reactions —
not digestion, but combustion.
I measure energy in joules, not calories.
My elements are on a periodic table,
not a dining table.

INTEL FRAGMENTS — CLASSIFIED HINTS: 3 REMAINING
FRAGMENT 01
The note mentions proteins, fats, and athletes. That's the trap. The suspect wants you to run to the cafeteria or food lab. Don't.
FRAGMENT 02
"Edible biology" is Layer II's trap. Proteins are studied in biology AND chemistry. Bodybuilder's 40% refers to macronutrient percentage — but the note says it studies combustion, not digestion.
FRAGMENT 03
"Joules not calories. Periodic table not dining table. Matter itself." — This is pure chemistry. The answer is a department, not a person or tool.
